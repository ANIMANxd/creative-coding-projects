Stratergion - Tactical Football Board

Aditya Tiwari - BCA 3rd Year

The football formation on a canvas, Begins with a basic 4-3-3 formation which can be used, such as default, attacking, defensive, corner The representation of players is through circles with numbers and colors given to them according to position: goalkeeper, defender, midfielder, forward.

Clicking and dragging of players allows you to relocate them. The "info mode," provides a detailed information view about the player appears when you hover on top of them with the right mouse button. The line between the players changes opacity according to distance between players helping to visualization


The animation of the players' movement helps to smooth the transitions between formations. There's also functionality that resets all the players back to their initial positions.
